Kellogg's Course Bidding System

...is suboptimal at best and broken at worst. It's time for a reboot.

What's the purpose of this site?

This site strives to educate people on the structure and pitfalls of Kellogg's current course bidding system. In short, most Kellogg students are unable to take all of the courses they wish to before graduating, largely due to inflated costs and limited availability.

Once you're acquainted with the course bidding system and its shortcomings, I invite you to read about my thoughts on how the system could be improved and you can decide if agree (spoiler alert: you probably will).
